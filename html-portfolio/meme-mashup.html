Meme Mashup

An assignment where we were asked to take two different memes (Yes, you heard that right) and make one NEW meme.

The first thing that came to mind were these two memes- the "I'm in Danger" from the Simpsons, and "This is Fine," which is from a comic excerpt. This edit was done through a mixture of Adobe Photoshop (combining the images) and MediBang Paint (to create the details on the windows and the inside of the bus)
